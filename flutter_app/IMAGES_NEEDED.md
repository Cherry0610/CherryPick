## ğŸ’¡ Recommendations

### For Product Images:
1. **Use a placeholder service** initially:
   - `https://via.placeholder.com/400x400?text=Product`
   - Or use `https://picsum.photos/400/400` for random images

2. **Later integration:**
   - Fetch product images from retailer APIs
   - Use product image URLs from your backend

### For Store Logos:
1. **Download from official sources:**
   - Visit each retailer's website
   - Look for press/media kits
   - Download official logos

2. **Or create simple text-based logos:**
   - Use store name with custom font
   - Black text on white background (matches theme)

### For Onboarding:
1. **Create simple illustrations:**
   - Use tools like Figma, Canva, or Adobe Illustrator
   - Keep it simple: black and white line art
   - Or use icon-based illustrations

2. **Or use screenshots:**
   - Take actual screenshots of your app
   - Add annotations/arrows showing features
